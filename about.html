Our History

Founded in 2010, Consulting Columbus was born from the vision of a group of professionals passionate about innovation and committed to transforming the way companies operate in the market. From the beginning, our focus has been to deeply understand our clients' needs and develop solutions that add real value to their businesses.

In the early years, we concentrated on offering personalized consulting services, helping companies optimize their processes and increase their operational efficiency. As we gained recognition in the market, we expanded our portfolio to include a wider range of services, from software development to digital marketing strategies.

In 2015, we reached a significant milestone by establishing our first international branch, expanding our capacity to serve clients across various continents. This expansion was accompanied by the constant growth of our team, composed of highly qualified professionals dedicated to achieving excellence in everything we do.

Today, Consulting Columbus is a reference in the industry, recognized for its ability to innovate and its unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We continue to evolve and adapt to market changes, always aiming to provide solutions that make a difference for our partners.
