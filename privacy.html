Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Consulting Columbus, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your data when you visit our website or use our services.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We recommend that you read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices.

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services for you. The information we collect includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, and other contact information.
  • Usage Information: Data about how you use our website and services, including pages visited, time spent on pages, and actions taken.

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser, and other technical information.

We may also collect additional information as necessary to provide a better and more personalized service.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for various purposes, including:

  • Provision and Maintenance of Services: To operate and maintain our services.

We may also use your information for other purposes compatible with the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Sharing

  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, the safety of our users, or to respond to an emergency.

In all circumstances, we will take steps to ensure that your information is adequately protected.

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the security of your personal information. We use encryption technology and strict security practices to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
